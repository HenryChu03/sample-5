Dog Breeds

Shih Tzu The Shih Tzu is a very adorable dog, it is covered with so much hair on it's coat that they look like a lion. It is known to be friendly around children, they make great pets if you love hanging out. They are in the middle of having a high energy and the energy of a couch potato, and they'll find their own ways to keep themselves entertained.
Golden Retriever The Golden Retriever is a very popular dog breed in America. They can aid in hunting and can be great guides for the blind and for search-and-rescue. They are very friendly and playful.
German Shepherd The German Shepherd is a smart dog with a muscular physicality. Their energy is so high you can practically play so many games with them. It needs a job or activity for mental stimulation.
